Digital transformation consultants first come into a company with the purpose of understanding how it works and its current processes. They then begin to provide suggestions and recommendations on new technology and applications that should replace existing legacy tools and processes. From there they assist companies during the migration, implementation, and adoption phases of digital transformation.
